Before You Book

School window film — your questions answered.

Is window tinting worth it for a school?+
Yes — schools have large classroom, hall and stairwell glass that traps heat and throws glare across whiteboards and screens. Quality film removes that glare so students can actually see the board, drops indoor temperatures up to 10°C for more comfortable focused learning, blocks 99% of UV, and adds safety film that holds shattered glass together around children.
How much does school window tinting cost in Thailand?+
Installed film runs from ฿70/sq ft for carbon film up to ฿180/sq ft for premium sputtering. Because classroom blocks and halls carry large glass areas, most school projects are quoted per building or phase — the exact figure depends on total glass area, glass height and film tier, confirmed free during your on-site measurement.
Does window film make classroom glass safer for students?+
Yes. Safety film (0.4–0.8mm) bonds to the glass so that if a pane is struck or breaks, the fragments are held in place instead of showering into the room. On large classroom and hall windows around children, this shatter-hold layer is one of the main reasons schools choose film.
Will window film make the classroom too dark to see the board?+
No. We fit crystal-clear, high-clarity film that cuts glare and heat without darkening the room. Students see the whiteboard and projector screens clearly because the harsh reflected glare is removed while natural daylight is kept — the room stays bright, just cooler and more comfortable.
How does window film lower a school's electricity bill?+
Premium ceramic and sputtering films reject the majority of heat and 99% of UV, lowering classroom temperatures by up to 10°C and cutting A/C load 20–30%. Across a whole campus of large glass that's a measurable reduction in the monthly electricity bill, especially for sun-facing classrooms and halls.
Do you install school window film on-site across Thailand?+
Yes — JaiDeeClear is a fully mobile service. Certified technicians come to your school in Phuket, Bangkok, Chonburi and beyond. We schedule around your timetable, including weekends and term breaks, and free on-site measurement is included with every quote.
