Why It Matters

Why UV protection matters.

Thailand's climate demands serious solar protection — for your health, your interiors and your energy bills.

Three core challenges we solve

  • Ultraviolet radiation damages interiors — fading furniture, flooring and artwork over time.
  • Glare affects comfort and productivity in workspaces, reducing quality of life and efficiency.
  • Privacy concerns grow in developments with expansive windows and open floor plans.
JaiDeeClear UV-protection films block up to 99% of harmful UV rays whilst delivering energy savings of up to 30%.

Maintain crystal-clear views, protect your interior furniture and reduce electricity costs.
