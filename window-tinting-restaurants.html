Before You Book

Café & restaurant window film — your questions answered.

Is window tinting worth it for a restaurant or café?+
Yes — a café's frontage glass is its biggest asset and its biggest heat problem. The window seats are the best seats and the hottest, so guests move away from them by midday. Quality film rejects the infrared heat and 99% of UV, keeps diners comfortable by the glass, protects timber, upholstery and menus from fade, and cuts glare on your frontage — all without darkening the room or hiding your interior from the street.
How much does café window tinting cost in Thailand?+
Installed film runs from ฿70/sq ft for carbon film up to ฿180/sq ft for premium sputtering. A typical café or restaurant frontage is 100–300 sq ft of glass, so most projects fall between ฿7,000 and ฿55,000 depending on total glass area and film tier. The exact figure is confirmed free during your on-site measurement.
Will window film make my café too dark?+
No. We fit optically-clear ceramic and carbon films that cut heat, glare and UV while keeping the space bright and the view to the street clear. The frontage still reads open and inviting — film removes the harsh glare, not the light. We match the tier to how much privacy and heat control you want, room by room.
Can you install without closing the restaurant?+
Yes. We work early mornings, after close, or between services and finish most café and restaurant frontages in a single day. No glass is removed and there's no workshop visit, so you rarely lose a trading day. We schedule the install around your service.
Does window film stop glare on the frontage?+
Yes — cutting glare is one of the biggest wins for cafés. Afternoon sun bouncing off frontage glass washes out your interior, your signage and diners' screens. Our film reduces that glare sharply so the space stays comfortable and photographs well, while the street view stays crystal clear.
Do you install café and restaurant window film on-site across Thailand?+
Yes — JaiDeeClear is a fully mobile service. Certified technicians come to your café or restaurant in Bangkok, Phuket, Chonburi and beyond. No glass is removed and free on-site measurement is included with every quote.
