Phuket's Climate Challenge

Why Phuket Properties
Need Window Film

Phuket sits at 8°N with coastal UV that regularly exceeds index 13 — amplified by reflection off the Andaman Sea. Modern villa architecture maximises glass for sea views, turning your living room into a solar collector that fades furniture, heats up rapidly, and drives up electricity bills.

☀️

Coastal UV Index 13

UV amplified by sea reflection makes Phuket one of Thailand's most UV-intense environments. Unprotected glass lets this radiation fade furniture, damage skin, and degrade flooring year-round.

🌡️

38°C+ Tropical Heat

Phuket's humidity-amplified heat and large villa glass areas create greenhouse conditions. Window film cuts incoming solar heat by up to 60%, dramatically reducing how hard your AC has to work.

🌀

Monsoon Season Storms

From May to October, Phuket's monsoon brings heavy rain and strong winds. Safety window film holds glass together during storms — an additional layer of protection for your villa's large glass panels.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about window tinting in Phuket.

How much does window tinting cost in Phuket? +
Window tinting in Phuket ranges from ฿50–70/sq.ft for carbon film to ฿155+/sq.ft for premium sputtering film. A typical 3-bedroom villa costs ฿15,000–45,000 depending on glass area and film choice. We provide free on-site measurements and quotes.
Do you cover all areas of Phuket island? +
Yes — we serve the entire island including Patong, Kata, Karon, Kamala, Bang Tao, Cherng Talay, Rawai, Chalong, Phuket Town, Kathu, Wichit, Rassada, and Cape Panwa. Our technicians travel to your property for free.
How long does installation take? +
A typical condo takes 3–5 hours. A 3–4 bedroom villa takes one full day. Larger resort or commercial projects take 1–3 days. You can stay in the property throughout installation.
Which film do you recommend for Phuket? +
For most Phuket homes and condos, our Ceramic Nano film offers the best balance of heat rejection, UV protection, and clarity — it preserves sea views while blocking intense coastal UV. For large west-facing windows, Ceramic UV400 provides extended UV spectrum blocking. For luxury villas and resort properties, our Sputtering Series delivers maximum performance.
How much will I save on electricity? +
Most Phuket customers see a 20–30% reduction in monthly electricity bills. Properties with large glass areas and heavy AC usage see the biggest savings — some villa owners in Rawai and Kamala report saving ฿2,000–4,000+ per month during hot season.
Is window film effective during Phuket's monsoon season? +
Yes. Film provides year-round benefits — it blocks solar heat during Phuket's hot season (February–May) and continues blocking UV even on overcast monsoon days (May–October). UV rays penetrate cloud cover, so protection is consistent regardless of weather. Safety films also hold glass in place during monsoon storms.
What's the warranty on your window film? +
All films carry a manufacturer-backed warranty: 2 years for Carbon Series, 5 years for Ceramic Nano and Ceramic UV400, and 7 years for Sputtering Series. Covers peeling, bubbling, discoloration, and delamination.
Can window film be removed or replaced? +
Yes — window film is fully removable. We strip the old film and install new. Old film removal costs ฿20–30/sq.ft additional.
