Before You Book

Window tinting in Chonburi — your questions answered.

How much does window tinting cost in Chonburi?+
Window tinting in Chonburi runs from ฿70/sq ft for carbon film up to ฿180+/sq ft for premium sputtering. A typical 2-bedroom Pattaya or Jomtien condo costs ฿5,400–11,000 depending on glass area and film choice. Every quote includes a free on-site measurement anywhere across the Eastern Seaboard.
Which areas of Chonburi do you cover?+
We cover the whole province and Eastern Seaboard — Pattaya, Jomtien, Na Jomtien, Pratumnak Hill, Wong Amat, Naklua, Bang Lamung, Sri Racha, Laem Chabang, Bang Saray, Sattahip, Ban Chang and Chonburi town, plus the Amata City Chonburi and Amata industrial estates. Our technicians travel to your property for free measurement and installation.
Do you handle commercial and industrial projects like Amata estates and offices?+
Yes. Alongside condos and villas, we film offices, showrooms, hotels and factories across Sri Racha, Laem Chabang and the Amata City Chonburi industrial estates. We use commercial-grade film, work around your operating hours, and can phase larger sites over several days with zero disruption.
Which window film is best for Pattaya's beachfront condos?+
For most Pattaya and Jomtien sea-view condos, our Ceramic Nano film is the top choice — it blocks intense coastal heat and glare while preserving the panoramic sea views that make these properties valuable. West-facing units benefit from Ceramic UV400, and hotels or commercial towers usually specify our Sputtering Series for maximum performance.
How much will I save on electricity with window film?+
Most Chonburi customers see a 20–30% reduction in monthly electricity bills. Beachfront condos and pool villas with heavy AC usage see the biggest savings — some owners in Jomtien and Pratumnak report saving ฿1,500–3,500+ per month during hot season.
Is window film good for retirement homes on the Eastern Seaboard?+
Absolutely. Many of our Chonburi customers are retirees who want comfortable indoor temperatures without running the AC at full blast all day. Film reduces heat gain, lowers electricity costs and blocks 99% of harmful UV — real health and lifestyle benefits for long-term residents in Pattaya, Jomtien and Bang Saray.
