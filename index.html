Before You Book

Window tinting, answered.

How much does window tinting cost in Thailand?+
Installed rates run from ฿70/sq ft for our budget carbon film up to ฿180/sq ft for premium sputtering ceramic. Final price depends on total glass area, window height, and film tier — confirmed free during your on-site measurement. Use the calculator above for an instant estimate.
How long does installation take?+
Most condos and offices are completed in a single day. Large villas may take two. We schedule around you and leave zero mess behind.
Will the film damage my windows or glass?+
No. Our films are professionally applied and fully removable. They're designed to protect your glass — including safety film that holds shattered glass in place.
What warranty do I get?+
Every install comes with a written warranty — 5 years on Essential film, 7 years on Performance, and lifetime-grade cover on Elite. Genuine premium film only.
Do you really come to my property?+
Yes — we're a fully on-site service across 7 Thai cities. Free measurement is included with every quote. No need to remove windows or visit a workshop.
