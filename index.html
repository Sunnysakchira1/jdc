Understanding Film Performance Ratings

UVR — Ultraviolet Rejection: Percentage of harmful UV rays blocked
IRR — Infrared Rejection: Percentage of heat-causing infrared blocked
TSER — Total Solar Energy Rejected: Overall solar heat control capability
