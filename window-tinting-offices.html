Before You Book

Office window film — your questions answered.

Is window tinting worth it for an office?+
Yes — offices run screens all day behind large glass façades, so glare, heat and A/C cost are constant. Quality window film removes glare on monitors and in meeting rooms, cuts indoor heat by up to 10°C on glass-heavy floors, lowers A/C running costs 20–30%, and gives daytime privacy to boardrooms — all without darkening the workspace.
How much does office window tinting cost in Thailand?+
Installed film runs from ฿70/sq ft for carbon film up to ฿180/sq ft for premium sputtering. A typical office floor carries around 200–500 sq ft of glass, so most single-floor projects fall between ฿15,000 and ฿70,000. The exact figure depends on total glass area, window height and film tier — confirmed free during your on-site measurement.
Will window film reduce screen glare in meeting rooms?+
Yes. Optically-clear solar film cuts harsh glare on monitors, projectors and glass conference tables without dimming the room. Staff stop squinting or closing blinds, and presentations stay readable even on west-facing floors that catch the full afternoon sun.
Does office window film give daytime privacy for boardrooms?+
Daytime-privacy film reflects the brighter side, so during the day people outside can't see into meeting rooms and boardrooms while your team keeps the view out. At night — when interior lights are on and it's darker outside — that reverses, so we recommend pairing film with blinds where round-the-clock privacy is required, and advise the right tier per room during measurement.
How much can office window film cut energy bills?+
Premium ceramic and sputtering films reject the majority of infrared heat and 99% of UV, lowering indoor temperatures by up to 10°C and cutting A/C load 20–30%. Across a glass-heavy floor running air-conditioning all day, that's a measurable drop in monthly electricity cost — the film often pays for itself over a few seasons.
Do you install office window film on-site across Thailand?+
Yes — JaiDeeClear is a fully mobile service. Certified technicians come to your office in Bangkok, Phuket, Chonburi and beyond. No glass is removed and installs can be phased around working hours or done out of hours. Free on-site measurement is included with every quote.
