Positioning & voice

Who we are

Professional window film, installed at your door.

JaiDeeClear fits premium UV-protection and heat-rejection window film on-site across Thailand — for buildings, residences and yachts. Phuket-born, bilingual Thai/English, quality-obsessed.

BuildingResidenceYachts

Voice — do

  • Benefit-first, plain and confident: "Keep the view, lose the heat."
  • Short punchy lines: "Less heat. Lower bills. Protected furniture."
  • Concrete & specific ("up to 10°C cooler", "Ceramic UV 400-17", "99% UV").
  • Bilingual-ready — Thai + English, both first-class.
  • Emoji as light seasoning on social (☀️🕶️💡) — sparingly on the website.

Voice — don't

  • No jargon dumps or spec sheets without the benefit.
  • No hype or fake urgency ("BEST EVER!!!").
  • Don't bury the offer — free on-site quote is always one tap away.
  • Don't mix currencies or units — ฿ and sq ft, consistently.
  • No stock imagery where a real install photo exists.

08

Imagery

Real installs only — technicians in JaiDeeClear shirts, glass with sky/sea/villa behind it, the before/after temperature story. Warm, natural light. The blue that appears is the glass itself, not a filter. Short install videos (muted autoplay loops) over static hero images where we have them.

Imagery — do

  • Real JaiDeeClear projects (Sava Beach Villas, Anchan Flora, Botanica Foresta…).
  • Show the view being kept — sea, pool, skyline through the glass.
  • Install-in-progress shots build trust (branded shirts, squeegee, film roll).
  • Muted autoplay video loops for hero / proof moments.

Imagery — don't

  • No generic stock windows or western/unbranded crews.
  • No heavy filters that fake the tint result.
  • No dark, mirror-limo-style "car tint" vibe — this is architectural.
  • Don't crop out the JaiDeeClear branding on shirts.
